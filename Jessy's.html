How it's actually made

From harvest to your fridge

1

Harvest & shred

Sea moss is sun-dried on the coast; cabbage is hand-shredded the same morning we ferment it. Nothing sits in a warehouse.

2

Soak or salt

Moss is soaked and washed until soft; cabbage is layered with salt and packed tight to push out air.

3

Blend or ferment

Moss is blended into gel and chilled same-day. Kraut is left to ferment under weight, untouched.

4

Taste, jar

Every batch is tasted before it's jarred.
